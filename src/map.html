Map & Direction

The entire Bodrum peninsula is peppered with numerous historical sites, including the renovated Bodrum Castle and the Museum of Underwater Archaeology. In Bodrum city a glitzy marina, is balanced out by quaint cobbled back-streets and the multitude of restaurants, bars, shops and cultural events make this a great year round destination, but which really comes alive in spring.
